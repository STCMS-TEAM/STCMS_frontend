Frequently Asked Questions

To create a tournament, click on "Create Tournaments" from the home page or navigate to the Create Tournament page. Fill in the tournament details including name, description, start and end dates, select the tournament type (Single Elimination, Double Elimination, or Round Robin), and choose the sport. Once you submit the form, your tournament will be created and you can start adding teams and players.

You can browse available tournaments by clicking on "Join Tournament" or "View tournaments" from the home page. This will take you to the tournaments page where you can filter tournaments by sport (Soccer, Basketball, or Volleyball). Select a tournament to view its details, matches, and standings. To participate, you'll need to register a team for that tournament.

To register a team, go to "Manage Players & Teams" from the home page or use the "Create Team" option in the navigation menu (when logged in). Select the sport for your team, then fill in the team name and add player email addresses. You can add starting players and substitutes. Once your team is created, you can join tournaments that match your team's sport.

Click on "Track Live Results" or "View Results" to see all available tournaments. Select a sport category and then choose a tournament to view its matches. You can filter matches by status: All, Live (in progress), Concluded (completed), or Scheduled (upcoming). The results page shows match scores, team standings, and tournament progress in real-time.
